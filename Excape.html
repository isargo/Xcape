Directions

The green filled square is your character. Click on the arrow buttons to move your character. Get to the pink filled square to win the game.

DO NOT touch the red squares or the game starts over.

Race with other people to have fun. Once you get to the green outlined square, you get a point. First to 5 points win. Or go for the 10!

The first upgrade is reached when your score gets to 500! The second is reached when your score gets to 750! The third is at 900 points!
